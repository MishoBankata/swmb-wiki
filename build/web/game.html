Journey to Ilum

made by MishoBankata

You are a Jedi Padawan in the final stages of their training. To complete your training and become a true Jedi,
you will need to craft a lightsaber.
You already have your hilt, but the lightsaber
needs a power source - a kyber crystal.

Like every other Jedi, you must travel to the frozen planet Ilum
and search for a crystal in the planet's caves.
Ilum is rich in kyber crystals, but not just any crystal will do.
You must use the Force to find the one right for you.
The Jedi Council's orders were clear - return with a kyber crystal,
May the Force be with you...

Use WASD or the or the buttons on the screen to move your character.
You can fight the monsters, but you can't escape them.
Find your Kyber crystal to win.
